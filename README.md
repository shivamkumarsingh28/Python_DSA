 Python DSA Course topics include:

1. Introduction to Data Structures and Algorithms: This section covers the basics of data structures and algorithms, including their definitions, types, and applications. It also introduces the Python programming language and its features.

2. Arrays: This section covers the fundamentals of arrays, including their definition, operations, and applications. It also covers various array sorting algorithms such as selection sort, insertion sort, bubble sort, merge sort, quick sort, heap sort, etc.

3. Linked Lists: This section covers the fundamentals of linked lists, including their definition, operations, and applications. It also covers various linked list sorting algorithms such as selection sort, insertion sort, bubble sort, merge sort etc.

4. Stacks & Queues: This section covers the fundamentals of stacks & queues including their definition, operations and applications. It also covers various stack & queue sorting algorithms such as selection sort , insertion sort , bubble sort , merge sort etc. 

5. Trees: This section covers the fundamentals of trees including their definition , operations , traversals and applications . It also covers various tree sorting algorithms such as selection sort , insertion sort , bubble sort , merge sort etc . 

6. Graphs: This section covers the fundamentals of graphs including their definition , operations , traversals and applications . It also covers various graph sorting algorithms such as selection sort , insertion sort , bubble sort , merge sort etc . 

7. Searching & Hashing: This section covers the fundamentals of searching & hashing including their definition , operations and applications . It also covers various search & hash sorting algorithms such as linear search , binary search , hash table lookup etc . 

8. Advanced Algorithms: This section covers advanced topics in data structures & algorithms such as dynamic programming , greedy algorithm design techniques etc .
